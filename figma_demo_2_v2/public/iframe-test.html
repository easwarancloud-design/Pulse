Test Application with Embedded Pulse

This page demonstrates how the PulseEmbedded component works within an iframe in another application.

  2. Watch dropdown breakout: Notice how the dropdown appears outside the iframe on this parent page
  3. Click predefined questions: Click on any of the blue suggestion buttons
